ABOUT ME

Emily Williams is a junior multi-platform journalism major with a minor in French studies at the University of Maryland. She is currently a staff reporter covering Prince George's County for the Diamondback, an independent student newspaper at the University of Maryland. Previously, she worked as a reporting intern with the Hyattsville Life & Times and the College Park Here & Now.
